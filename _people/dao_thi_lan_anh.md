Lan Anh is a third-year undergraduate student majoring in English Studies with a focused interest in Applied Linguistics and the multifaceted roles of language in social life. She views language not merely as a method of communication, but as a dynamic system that shapes our understanding of human experience and the world around us. In her academic perspective, nearly every aspect of life can be interpreted, expressed, and connected through the lens of language.

Currently at the beginning of her research journey, Lan Anh is actively pursuing opportunities to engage in academic projects that explore the application of linguistic theory in community contexts, language education, and the social functions of communication. Her research interests include language acquisition, sociolinguistics, and the development of evidence-based strategies to support language learning and community empowerment.

Lan Anh’s long-term academic goal is to contribute to the advancement of language education methodologies that are grounded in rigorous research and that generate meaningful impact within Vietnamese society.
